Ingredients

  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tsp sunflower oil
  • 1tsp butter

Steps

  1. Season the beaten eggs well with salt and pepper
  2. Pour the eggs into the pan
  3. Tilt the pan again to allow it to fill back up with the runny eggs.
  4. Fill the omlette with whatever you like
